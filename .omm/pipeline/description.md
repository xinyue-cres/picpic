Feature-extraction and verdict pipeline. Photos flow through six analyzer stages, then rules. Each stage reads rows needing work from SQLite and writes back into columns of the same photos table — SQLite is the coordination surface, not an in-memory queue. Phase 1 stages (hashes, EXIF, blur, similar) always run; CLIP is optional and gated by the [clip] extra plus the presence of categories.yml. Rules is the last stage and produces verdict/verdict_reason without consuming clip_labels.
