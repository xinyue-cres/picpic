Argparse subcommand hierarchy exposed by the `picpic` entry point. Every subcommand takes a required `library` positional argument (validated via _require_library: must exist and be a directory). analyze and all share --no-clip/--clip-only (mutually exclusive) and --force-clip. serve accepts --host (default 127.0.0.1), --port (default 8765), --no-open. categories requires exactly one of --list/--check/--init.
