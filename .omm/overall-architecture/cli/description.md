Argparse-based CLI dispatcher. Exposes six subcommands: scan, analyze, rules, all (chained scan+analyze+rules), serve (web UI), categories (list/check/init YAML taxonomy). Each subcommand validates library.exists() and is_dir() before opening the DB. analyze/all accept --no-clip and --clip-only as a mutually-exclusive group, plus --force-clip. categories requires exactly one of --list/--check/--init.
