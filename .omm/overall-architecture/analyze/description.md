Feature extraction group. runner.py orchestrates five passes over active photos: hashes (file_hash, phash), exif (dimensions, camera, screenshot heuristic), blur (Laplacian variance), similar (phash Hamming clustering into dup_group), clip (zero-shot classification against categories.yml). CLIP pass is optional and gated by the [clip] extra; failure logs to stderr and preserves Phase 1 results.
